Our Story

A Vision Born From the Sea

Luce Mare was born from a deep love for the Mediterranean — its colors, its light, and above all, its cuisine. Executive Chef Marco Bellini spent over a decade refining his craft across Michelin-starred kitchens in Barcelona, Monaco, and Amalfi before founding this restaurant.

Every dish is a dialogue between tradition and modernity — seasonal ingredients sourced from sustainable producers, prepared with obsessive technique and presented with understated elegance.
